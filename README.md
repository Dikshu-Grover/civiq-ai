# CIVIQ AI

### AI-Powered Disaster Intelligence & Decision Support Platform for India

> **CIVIQ AI** is an intelligent disaster command center designed to support emergency management through AI-assisted situational awareness, risk assessment, and operational decision support. By combining interactive visualizations, predictive intelligence, and explainable AI recommendations, the platform demonstrates how modern AI can assist authorities in preparing for and responding to natural disasters.

---

## Overview

Emergency response requires timely information, coordinated decision-making, and the ability to rapidly assess changing conditions. CIVIQ AI consolidates disaster intelligence into a unified operational dashboard where users can monitor regional risks, explore disaster scenarios, and receive AI-generated recommendations.

The platform is designed as a proof of concept for a next-generation Emergency Operations Center (EOC), showcasing how Generative AI can augment—not replace—human decision-making during disaster management.

---

## Key Capabilities

### National Operations Dashboard

A centralized command interface providing a comprehensive operational view of disaster activity across India.

**Features include:**

* Interactive map of India
* Live operational metrics
* AI-generated Situation Summary
* State and district search
* High-Risk Regions panel
* Responsive command-center interface

---

### AI Disaster Intelligence

For every selected state or district, CIVIQ AI generates structured intelligence including:

* Overall Risk Assessment
* Confidence Score
* Risk Explanation
* 24-Hour Disaster Outlook
* Operational Priority
* Recommended Response Actions

The AI is designed to function as a disaster intelligence analyst by producing concise, explainable, and actionable insights.

---

### Regional Intelligence Panel

Selecting a location reveals a detailed operational overview including:

* Disaster Summary
* Weather Conditions
* Rainfall Information
* Flood Risk Assessment
* Heatwave Risk
* Water Scarcity Risk
* Healthcare Facilities
* Relief Shelters
* Emergency Resources
* AI Recommendations

Where live data is unavailable, representative sample data is used to demonstrate intended functionality.

---

### Decision Support Simulator

The simulation engine enables users to evaluate operational scenarios before implementation.

Supported scenarios include:

* Increased rainfall
* Road network disruption
* Additional rescue team deployment

The platform dynamically recalculates:

* Overall Risk
* Estimated Population Impact
* Response Time
* Resource Allocation
* AI Operational Recommendations

This demonstrates how AI can assist emergency planners by evaluating the potential consequences of operational decisions.

---

### Live Emergency Demonstration

The application includes a guided disaster response simulation illustrating a complete emergency workflow.

Simulation sequence:

1. Severe rainfall escalation
2. Rising flood risk
3. AI anomaly detection
4. Emergency alert generation
5. Rescue team deployment
6. Shelter activation
7. Resource coordination
8. AI-generated Situation Report

The demonstration highlights the end-to-end capabilities of an AI-assisted disaster command center.

## Disclaimer

CIVIQ AI is a demonstration platform developed for research, educational, and hackathon purposes.

Certain operational metrics, disaster intelligence, forecasts, emergency resources, and recommendations are generated using AI and representative sample datasets to demonstrate the capabilities of an AI-assisted disaster management system. The platform is **not intended for deployment in live emergency response environments**.
